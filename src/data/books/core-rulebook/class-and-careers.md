# Class and Careers

## Overview

Every Career belongs to one of 8 Classes and has 4 Career Levels, each better than the last. New characters always start at level 1 of their chosen Career. A Career Level lists its Status (Tier and Standing), 8 Skills, 4 Talents, and Trappings.

### Advance Scheme

Each Career Level's Advance Scheme marks 3 of the 10 Characteristics as advanceable at that level (a 4th unlocks at level 2, silver at level 3, gold at level 4 — see the character-creation chapter for the full mechanic). You may advance any Skill listed at your current Career Level or lower. Talents are only purchasable while you are at the Career Level that lists them. The Skill marked in italics in a Career's first level is its Earning Skill, used for the Earning downtime activity.

### Changing Career and Status

Completing a Career Level requires the listed number of Advances (5/10/15/20 for levels 1–4) in all 3 of its Characteristics and in 8 of its Skills, plus at least 1 Talent from that level. Changing to a new level or Career costs 100 XP if your current level is complete, 200 XP if not; moving to a different Class costs a further +100 XP.

Status is a Tier (Brass, Silver, or Gold) and a Standing (usually 1–5), set by your current Career Level. Higher Tier beats any Standing in a lower Tier; within a Tier, higher Standing wins. Status modifies Charm, Intimidate, Gossip, and Leadership Tests against people of different Tiers, and determines Earning income (see the character-creation chapter's Status Tier table for the coin formula).
